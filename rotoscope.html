About Rotoscoping

Rotoscoping is a frame-by-frame animation technique where animators trace over footage frame by frame. This meticulous process allows for precise movement capture and creates smooth, realistic animation based on live-action references.

My rotoscope projects demonstrate skill in motion tracking, character animation, and attention to detail. Each frame is carefully crafted to capture the essence of movement and emotion.

About Me

Hi, I'm John Luis N. Pilar — a 20-year-old aspiring 3D artist and a student at the Technological University of the Philippines Taguig Campus. Born on November 29, 2005, I've always been drawn to the worlds that games and films can create, and that passion drives me to build them myself.

I specialize in asset creation, environment art, and 3D modeling. My dream is to become a professional 3D artist, crafting immersive worlds and detailed assets that bring stories to life. I spend most of my free time studying VFX channels, 3D art tutorials, and playing AAA games — always analyzing how the environments and characters are built.

This portfolio showcases my journey through rotoscoping, 3D rendering, and digital art. Every project here is a step toward mastering the craft.
